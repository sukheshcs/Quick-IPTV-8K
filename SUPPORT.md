# Support for Quick IPTV 8K

Thank you for choosing Quick IPTV 8K! Here is how you can get help or contribute if you run into any issues.

## 🐛 Bug Reports & Issue Tracking
If you encounter a bug, a playback error, or unexpected behavior in the application:
1. First, search our [existing GitHub Issues](../../issues) to see if someone else has already reported it or found a workaround.
2. If your issue is new, please [open a new Issue](../../issues/new). 
3. Include as much detail as possible, such as:
   - Your macOS version and device (Intel or Apple Silicon).
   - What type of playlist or portal you are using (M3U, Xtream, Stalker).
   - Any error messages displayed in the player.

## 💡 Feature Requests
Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it! 
Please open an issue and tag it as an `enhancement`, or start a discussion in our repository. Describe the feature thoroughly and explain how it would improve the IPTV experience.

## 📺 Playback & Stream Questions
Quick IPTV 8K is strictly a media player interface. **We do not provide, host, or supply any IPTV content, playlists, or channel subscriptions.** 

If you are experiencing buffering, missing channels, or "401/403 Unauthorized" errors, this is almost always an issue with your provider, your internet connection, or your playlist configuration, not the application itself. Please verify your stream links work in other standard players (like VLC) before reporting a playback bug here.

## 🤝 Community & Contact
For general questions, troubleshooting, or just to show off your setup, feel free to use GitHub Discussions or reach out to the project maintainers directly.

Thank you for supporting Quick IPTV 8K!
