# Privacy Policy for Quick IPTV 8K

**Effective Date:** March 28, 2026

Thank you for choosing Quick IPTV 8K ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our macOS application, Quick IPTV 8K (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or contact details. 

### B. User Content (Playlists and Media)
Quick IPTV 8K is a local media player. All playlists (such as M3U files), URLs, and streaming content you input into the App are processed locally on your device. We do not have access to, monitor, or track the content you stream or watch.

### C. Usage Data and Analytics
We do not use third-party analytics trackers inside the App. If you opt-in to sharing crash reports and analytics with Apple via your macOS system settings, Apple may share anonymized crash logs and app performance data with us. This data does not identify you personally and is used solely to improve the app's stability and performance.

## 2. How We Use the Information
Any anonymized crash data provided by Apple is used exclusively to:
- Identify and fix bugs.
- Improve the performance and stability of Quick IPTV 8K.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
All your preferences, settings, and playlists are stored locally on your Mac. We do not upload your data to any external servers. Since we do not collect personal data, your privacy remains strictly protected.

## 4. Third-Party Links and Services
The App allows you to access third-party content through URLs and playlists you provide. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, terms of use, or content of any third-party streams or services you choose to access through the App. Please review the privacy policies of the content providers you use.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your privacy while using the App, please contact us or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
